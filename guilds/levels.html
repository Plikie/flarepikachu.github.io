Guild Levels and EXP

After players complete a game, they will be awarded with Guild EXP along with other rewards. The Guild EXP gain formula is as follows:

  • Normal
    • 4 Guild EXP per wave.
    • 200 bonus EXP if cleared Wave 25.
  • Hard
    • 8 Guild EXP per wave.
    • 500 bonus EXP if cleared Wave 25.
  • Endless
    • 4 Guild EXP per wave.

Upon creation, guilds will start at level 0. The amount of Guild EXP needed to reach each level is listed in the chart below.
