Guild Chat

When a player joins a guild, they will have access to their guild chat. Players can use guild chat by typing /gchat <message>, or with the alias /gc <message>.

gif1 showing gchat exmaple

Players that wish to not see guild chat can do use the command /g toggle to toggle guild chat.

gif2 showing gtoggle example

Players with permissions to manage guild mutes can mute individual guild members with /g mute <username> <length-of-punishment>, provided that their rank is higher than the target guild member.

gif3 showing g mute example
