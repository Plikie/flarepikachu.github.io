Guilds

Guilds will be available during the initial PvE release. Mirroring it's Hypixel counterpart, Guilds allows aspiring players to form a coalition and work towards strengthening their guild together.

Guild Features that will be available upon the initial PvE release include:

  • Guild Commands
  • Guild Chat
  • Guild Levels and EXP
  • Guild Bank
  • Guild Upgrades
  • Guild Settings and Permissions
