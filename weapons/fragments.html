Legend Fragments

Legend Fragments are obtained by completing PvE games with a Legendary weapon equipped. The concept of Legend Fragments is that you are building your legend as you progress into the game.

Legend Fragments are currently used for purchasing Weapon Upgrades and may be featured in other costs in the future.

Current Legend Fragment rates are as follows:

Base Rate
  • +1 Legend Fragment per Wave.
  • +5 Legend Fragments per Prestige Level of Current Class, awarded every 25 waves.
  • +2 Legend Fragments per Prestige Level of all other classes, awarded every 25 waves.
  • Normal and Endless mode follow the Base Rate for Legend Fragments.
  • Easy mode receives 0.75x the base rate for Legend Fragments.
  • Hard mode receives 2x the base rate for Legend Fragments.
Guild Upgrade
  • +2 Legend Fragments per Tier, with the 9th Tier offering +4 Legend Fragments instead.
  • The Guild Upgrade bonus is NOT discounted in Easy mode.
  • The Guild Upgrade bonus is doubled in Hard mode.
