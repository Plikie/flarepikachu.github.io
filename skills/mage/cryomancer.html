Cryomancer

A defense-oriented Mage specialization that uses Ice spells to slow down enemies and to creative defensive barriers.

Frostbolt

Shoot a frostbolt that will shatter for 268.8 - 345.5 damage and slow by 25% for 7 seconds. A direct hit will cause the enemy to take an additional 15% extra damage.

Has an optimal range of 30 blocks.

Freezing Breath

Breathe cold air in a cone in front of you, dealing 422 to 585 damage to all enemies and slowing them by 35% for 4 seconds.

Time Warp

Activate to place a time rune on the ground. After 5 seconds, you will warp back to that location and restore 30% of your health.

Arcane Shield

Surround yourself with arcane energy, creating a shield that will absorb incoming damage for up to 50% of your maximum health. Lasts 6 seconds.

Ice Barrier

Surround yourself with a layer of cold air, reducing damage taken by 50%. While Ice Barrier is active, enemy melee hits will slow the attacker by 20% for 2 seconds. Lasts 6 seconds.
