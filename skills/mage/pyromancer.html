Pyromancer

A damage-oriented Mage specialization that uses destructive fire spells to obliterate enemies.

Fireball

Energy Cost: 70

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Shoot a fireball that will explode for 334.4 - 433.4 damage. A direct hit will cause the enemy to take an additional 15% extra damage. Has an optimal range of 50 blocks.

Flame Burst

Cooldown: 9.4s

Energy Cost: 60

Crit Chance: 25%

Crit Multiplier: 185%

Launch a flame burst that will explode for 557 - 753 damage. The Crit Chance increases by 1% for each travelled block, with a 100% Crit Chance maximum.

Time Warp

Cooldown: 28.2s

Energy Cost: 30

Activate to place a time rune on the ground. After 5 seconds, you will warp back to that location and restore 30% of your health.

Arcane Shield

Cooldown: 31.3s

Energy Cost: 40

Surround yourself with arcane energy, creating a shield that will absorb incoming damage for up to 50% of your maximum health. Lasts 6 seconds.

Inferno

Cooldown: 47s

Combust into a molten inferno, increasing your Crit Chance and Crit Multiplier by 30%. Lasts 18 seconds.
