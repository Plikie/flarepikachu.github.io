Warlords 2 PvE Skills & Upgrades Rogue Assassin
A pome is...

Assassin

A damage-oriented Rogue specialization that is a master of stealth, rapidly taking out any enemies who cross them.

Judgement Strike

Energy Cost: 70

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 185%

Strike the targeted enemy, dealing 326 - 441 damage. Every fourth attack is a guaranteed critical strike. Critical strikes temporarily increase your movement speed by 25% for 2 seconds.

Incendiary Curse

Cooldown: 8s

Energy Cost: 60

Crit Chance: 25%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Ignite the targeted area with a cross flame, dealing 408 - 552 damage. Enemies hit are blinded for 2 seconds.

Shadow Step

Cooldown: 12s

Energy Cost: 20

Crit Chance: 15%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Leap forward, dealing 466 - 598 damage to all enemies close on cast or when landing on the ground. You take reduced fall damage while leaping.

Shadow Step has reduced range when holding a Flag.

Soul Switch

Cooldown: 30s

Energy Cost: 40

Switch locations with an enemy, blinding them for 1.5 seconds. Has an optimal range of 13 blocks. Soul Switch has low vertical range.

Order of Eviscerate*

Cooldown: 50s

Energy Cost: 60

Cloak yourself for 7 seconds, granting you movement speed and making you invisible to the enemy for the duration. However, taking up to 600 fall damage or any type of ability damage will end your invisibility.

All your attacks against an enemy will mark them vulnerable. Vulnerable enemies take 130% more damage. Additionally, enemies hit from behind take an additional 140% more damage.

Killing your mark will reset both your Shadow Step and Order of Eviscerate's cooldown and refund the energy cost. Assisting in killing your mark will only refund half the cooldown.
