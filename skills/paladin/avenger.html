Warlords 2 PvE Skills & Upgrades Paladin Avenger
A pome is...

Avenger

A damage-oriented Paladin specialization that focuses on draining energy from enemies and has access to minor healing.

Avenger's Strike

Energy Cost: 90

Crit Chance: 25%

Crit Multiplier: 185%

Strike the targeted enemy, dealing 359-485 damage and removing 10 energy.

Consecrate

Cooldown: 7.8s

Energy Cost: 50

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Consecrate the ground below your feet, declaring it sacred. Enemies standing on it will take 158.4 - 213.6 damage per second and take 20% increased damage from your paladin strikes. Lasts 5 seconds.

Light Infusion

Cooldown: 15.7s

You become infused with light, restoring 120 energy and increasing your movement speed by 40% for 3 seconds.

Holy Radiance

Cooldown: 19.6s

Energy Cost: 20

Crit Chance: 15%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Radiate with holy energy, healing yourself and all nearby allies for 582 - 760 health.

You may look at an enemy to mark them for 8 seconds. Avenger's Mark reduces the targetted enemy's energy gain per second by 8.

Avenger's Mark has an optimal range of 15 blocks.

Avenger's Wrath

Cooldown: 52.8s

Burst with incredible holy power, causing your Avenger's Strikes to hit up to 2 additional enemies that are within 5 blocks of your target. Your energy gain per second is increased by 20 for the duration of Avenger's Wrath. Lasts 12 seconds.
