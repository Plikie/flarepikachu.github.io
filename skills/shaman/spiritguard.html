Spiritguard

A defense-oriented Shaman specialization that calls upon the aid of spirits old and new to mitigate damage and avoid death.

Fallen Souls

Energy Cost: 55

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 180%

Summon a wave of fallen souls, dealing 164 - 212 damage to all enemies they pass through. Each target hit reduces the cooldown of Spirit Link by 2 seconds.

Has a maximum range of 35 blocks.

Spirit Link

Cooldown: 8.6s

Energy Cost: 40

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Links your spirit with up to 3 enemy players, dealing 236.2 - 446.2 damage to the first target hit. Each additional hit deals 20% reduced damage. You gain 40% speed for 1.5 seconds, and take 15% reduced damage for 4.5 seconds.

Has an initial cast range of 20 blocks.

Soulbinding Weapon

Cooldown: 21.9s

Energy Cost: 30

Your melee attacks BIND enemies for 2 seconds. Against BOUND targets, your next Spirit Link will heal you for 400 health (half for 2 nearby allies). Your next Fallen Souls will reduce the cooldown of all abilities by 1.5s. (1s for 2 nearby allies). Both buffs may be activated for each melee hit. Lasts 12 seconds.

Successful soulbind procs will grant you 25% knockback resistance for 1.2 seconds. (Max 3.6 seconds).

Repentance

Cooldown: 31.32s

Energy Cost: 20

Taking damage empowers your damaging abilities and melee hits, restoring health and energy based on 10 + 10% of the damage you've recently took. Lasts 12 seconds.

Death's Debt

Cooldown: 70.5

Energy Cost: 20

Spirits' Respite: Place down a totem that delays 100% of incoming damage towards yourself. Transforms into Death's Debt after 4-6 seconds (increases with higher health), or when you exit its 10 block radius.

Death's Debt: Take 100% of the damage delayed by Spirit's Respite over 6 seconds. The totem will heal nearby allies for 15% of all damage that you take. If you survive, deal 15% of the damage delayed to nearby enemies.

Successful Soulbind procs on enemies add 0.5s to your totem duration. (Cap of 6s).
