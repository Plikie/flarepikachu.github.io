Masterworks Fair

As Common, Rare and Epic weapons aren’t all that exciting when you have Legendary weapons, the Masterworks Fair has been added to make obtaining weapons much more exciting. To explain the Masterworks Fair in a nutshell, players compete to submit a weapon with the highest weapon score. Players can check the weapon score of a weapon use /weaponscore to check their weapon score.

The Masterworks Fair is typically a weekly event that resets shortly after server restarts every Monday (10AM UTC). The first Masterworks Fair started right at the initial public release of Warlords 2 PvE.

There will be three separate competitions for each RNG-eligible rarity of Common, Rare and Epic. These competitions will run concurrently and players can submit weapons in all three categories during the same week. Players can submit multiple weapons per Masterworks Fair, but only the latest submitted one for each rarity will count.

Submitted weapons will NOT be returned to the player.

The Masterworks Fair is a closed submission contest. Players will not know the weapon scores of other people’s weapons unless other players choose to share their submitted weapon scores.

When the Masterworks Fair is over, players will receive rewards according to the ranking of their weapons by weaponscore when submissions are closed:

  • The top three submissions will receive a guaranteed Star Piece of the weapon rarity they submitted.
  • Each rarity awards a differeent amount of Supply Drop roll opportunities, with Common giving the least rolls while Epic giving the most rolls.
    • Common
    • 1st Place: 100 Susan Rolls
    • 2nd Place: 50 Susan Rolls
    • 3rd Place: 30 Susan Rolls
    • 4th to 10th Place: 20 Susan Rolls
    • 11th to 20th Place: 10 Susan Rolls
    • 21th Place and below: 5 Susan Rolls
    Rare
    • 1st Place: 150 Susan Rolls
    • 2nd Place: 75 Susan Rolls
    • 3rd Place: 50 Susan Rolls
    • 4th to 10th Place: 35 Susan Rolls
    • 11th to 20th Place: 20 Susan Rolls
    • 21th Place and below: 10 Susan Rolls
    Epic
    • 1st Place: 200 Susan Rolls
    • 2nd Place: 100 Susan Rolls
    • 3rd Place: 70 Susan Rolls
    • 4th to 10th Place: 50 Susan Rolls
    • 11th to 20th Place: 30 Susan Rolls
    • 21th Place and below: 20 Susan Rolls
  • There is a pity system for all fair rarities. You will be guaranteed the rewards for 4th to 10 Place if you submit weapons that pass the following thresholds:
    • Common: 90 Weapon Score
    • Rare: 85 Weapon Score
    • Epic: 75 Weapon Score
  • For more information about the Supply Drop, check out Supply Drop Susan.
