Guild Coins

Guild Coins are the currency used for unlocking and purchasing boosts for the guild.

Guild Coins are primarily gained by playing the game. Guild Coins are only rewarded if the player is in a guild. If multiple players from the same guild are playing together, each player will gain Guild Coins for that game instance.

2% of your coin gain is the default rate of your guild coin gain per game. For example, if a player earns 6,500 coins from a game, they will earn 130 (6,500 * 0.02) Guild Coins from that game instance.

Guild coin conversion rate can be upgraded. More information can be found here.
