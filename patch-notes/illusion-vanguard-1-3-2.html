Illusion Vanguard Hotfix - v1.3.2
Released December 5rd 2022
Wave Defense
Class Balance

General
  • Raised global speed cap from +100% to +150% max.

Mob Balance

Void Lancer
  • Void Cleanse has been adjusted as follows:
    • Inreased cooldown from 3 to 5 seconds.
    • Reduced the range from 6 to 5 blocks.
    • Reduced the taken duration from 2s to 1s.
    • Changed Void Cleanse to take duration from BUFF timers instead of ability timers. (This was the original intended behaviour.)

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Water Breath cone range upgrades not properly increasing range if the master was unlocked.
  • Fixed Shredding Fury (Windfury Master) not reducing damage resistance on hit.
