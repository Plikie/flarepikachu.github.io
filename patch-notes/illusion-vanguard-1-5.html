General

  • There is now a cap to how many weapons you can have.
    • For non-Patreons, this limit is 5 pages.
    • For Patreons, this limit is 10 pages.
  • If you exceed your weapon page limit, weapons will automatically be salvaged for shards at a 50% rate.
  • Players that have more weapons than the page limit allows will not have their weapons deleted, but any subsequent weapon will be auto-salvaged until they go under the weapon page limit.
  • You can now right click your weapon in the PvE menu to access it's configuration menu.
  • Added chat options for damage/healing/energy, allowing you to toggle these chat messages.
  • Increased guild role limit by 2 (to 7?) and cleaned up guild list.
  • Added a Mob Drop Menu in configuration menu.

Title Upgrades

You can now upgrade your title effects! For each weapon upgrade level you have, you can upgrade your weapon title effect the corresponding amount of times. For example, if your weapon is upgraded 2/4, you can upgrade your weapon title effect two times.

Weapon title upgrades are distinct to each title and weapon. Upgrading one title on your weapon does not unlock the upgraded effect for other titles and weapons you own.

You can upgrade your weapon title effects through the weapons menu by opening the config menu for your weapon and right click the name tag (where you used to select your titles).

Title effect upgrades will cost Zenith Stars along with coins, shards, and Legend Fragments.

  • Zenith Stars can be obtained by defeating Zenith in Normal, Hard, and Endless Mode (Low drop chance).
  • Limit Breakers are current planned to only be available from Event Rewards.

Class Balance

Pyromancer
  • Flame Awakening (Flame Burst Master) scaling based on distance has been reduced by roughly 20%.
  • Infernal Leap (Time Warp Master) will now grant you 40% movement speed along with 1% crit chance and crit multiplier for each block you travelled during Time Warp.
  • Dante's Inferno (Inferno Master) now increases duration by 0.25s for each crit as well.

Cryomancer
  • Frostbite Leap (Time Warp Master) will summon now a cryopod that draws nearby enemies towards your original warping point. You're granted 80% damage reduction for 5 seconds upon warping back. Additionally, all enemies near you will get shattered, slowing them by 80% for 3 seconds.

Aquamancer
  • Aquatic Assault (Water Bolt Master) now increases damage dealt by 10% instead of 15%.
  • Typhoon (Water Breath Master) now reduces cooldowns by 1.5s instead of 3s.
  • Monsoon Leap (Time Warp Master) will now leave a water altar when warping back that grants allies immunity to debuffs and slowness effects while near it. Lasts up to 10 seconds, based on how many blocks you travelled during Time Warp.

Avenger
  • Avenger's Slash (Avenger's Strike Master) will now hit an additional 2 targets. (Does not count as additional strikes for Avenger's Wrath.)

Protector

  • Holy Imbusion (Light Infusion Master) will grant the Protector 90% damage reduction/knockback resistance and immunity to all debuffs for 4 seconds in addition to resetting Holy Radiance's cooldown. (Note the previous effect was -30% cooldown, this is no longer the case. Effective downtime is 8s.)

Berserker

Revenant
