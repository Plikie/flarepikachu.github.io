Illusion Vanguard Hotfix - v1.5.1
Released February 19th 2023
Wave Defense
General

  • Mobs will spawn faster overall.

Endless

These changes were already live with v1.5 but we forgot to put them in the notes.

  • Player limit will remain at 6 players.
  • Insignia gain has been reverted back to 250 per kill, but insiginia gain per wave and boss wave completion has been halved.

Class Balance

Thunderlord
  • Thunderbolt (Lightning Rod Master) now increases damage dealt and speed by 40% for 12s instead of 8s.

Weapon Balance

Juggernaut
  • Juggernaut is now upgrade with a scale of 10/11.25/12.5/13.75/15% bonus damage/health.
  • The kills for each stack of Juggernaut has been changed from 100/200/300/400/500 to 100/200/400/800/1600.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Divine/Fervent only requiring 1 stack to activate their special effect.
  • Fixed Energy chat setting.
  • Fixed Narmer's Ancestors not having their proper skins equipped.
  • Fixed kiling Zenith sometimes causing the game to crash, resulting in no drops.
