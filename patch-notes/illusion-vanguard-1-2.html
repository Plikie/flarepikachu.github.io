Weapon Balance

Foreword

Hi. A couple days ago I mentioned to a couple peeps that we weren't planning on adjusting the stats of the Suspicious weapon.

After further consideration involving future content planning, many titles, including Suspicious, have been revamped. I will have a seperate page detailing the reasons of these changes available here.

Barring significant bugs and balance issues, we do not anticipate any more updates regarding the current titles in the near future. I have also (hopefully) updated all relevant pages affected by this update on the website.

Thank you for your understanding and have a good day!

~ Flare

Stacking Mechanic
  • Weapon abilities that utilize the stacking mechanic have no cooldown and can stack upon each other additively.
    • For example, two stacks of the Divine weapon (20% Damage per Stack) would result in a 40% Damage boost.
  • If the weapon ability stack is not maxed out, the weapon ability duration is renewed and the stack is incremented by 1.
  • If the weapon ability stack is maxed out, only the weapon ability duration is renewed.
  • (If coded correctly...) The name of the ability on action bar should display the ability name and the amount of stacks on the ability.
    • For example, 5 stacks of Requiem should be labeled as Requiem 5.

Activated Abilities
  • Weapon abilities that still run on a 30s cooldown are now referred to as active abilities.
  • There are currently two titles that have activated abilities, being Gale and Vigorous.
  • Activated Abilities can be activated by right-clicking your Nether Star in your 9th slot.

Divine
  • EPS: 7 -> 3
  • EPH: -13 -> -7
  • Rework the concept of Divine to be similar to Requiem/Fervent with a stacking system:
    • The effects of Divine stacks are additive to one another.
    • Divine can have a maximum of 3 stacks.
    • Gain a 20% Damage Boost for 30 seconds when you deal damage 40 times.

Enhanced
  • Enhanced now also adds active ability duration to all allies targetted by the title-holder's blue rune abilities (Slot 4).

Fervent
  • Rework the concept of Fervent to be similar to Requiem with a stacking system:
    • The effects of Fervent stacks are additive to one another.
    • Fervent can have a maximum of 3 stacks.
    • Gain a 20% Damage Boost for 30 seconds when you lose 5,000 health (take 5000 damage post damage reduction).

Requiem
  • Reworked the concept of Requiem interacting with Undying Army:
    • The effects of Requiem stacks are additive to one another.
    • Requiem can now have up to 5 stacks.
    • Requiem still lasts 60 seconds.
  • A stack of Requiem can be obtained:
    • When a teammate dies,
    • When a teammate has Undying Army applied to them, and
    • When a teammate pops under Undying Army.

Suspicious
  • Energy per Hit: 20 -> 3
  • Passive ability has been modified to:
    • Play an amogus sound and gain 20 energy whenever you land a melee crit

Stalwart
  • Added an additional passive ability to Stalwart:
    • If the player's HP is currently higher than 80% and the player will die from the next source of damage (e.g Narmer BOOM), set the player's health to 5% of their max HP and gain 99% damage reduction for 5 seconds. (Cooldown: 30s)

Vigorous
  • Energy per Second: 4 -> 2
  • Passive ability is now an activated ability with the following effect:
    • +20 Energy per second for 10 seconds. (Cooldown 30s)

Vorpal
  • Energy per Hit: 7 -> 4
  • Melee Damage: 180 - 200 -> 200 - 220

Class Balance

Protector
  • Alleviating Strike (Protector's Strike Master) now properly increases healing by 50% on lowest allies (previously 70%), and now also doubles healing range.

Revenant
  • Orbs of Life now targets the lowest ally upon sneaking/reactivating the ability.
