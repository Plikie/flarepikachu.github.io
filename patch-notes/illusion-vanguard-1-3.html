Endless

  • Increased overall reward gain by roughly 20%.
  • Illusion Crossfire has been completely revamped.
  • Waves after wave 50 now spawn every 5 seconds instead of 10.
  • You now get an additional ability tier upgrade at wave 25 and 75 respectively.
  • Reduced mob health scaling by 50%.
  • Mob damage now scales with waves completed.
    • Mobs will roughly deal 2x damage at wave 50 and 3x at wave 100 for reference. (Based on player count.)
  • New bosses have been added for wave 60, 70 and 100. (80 and 90 will follow in a later patch.)
  • Completely reworked mob distribution from wave 1 to 100 and beyond. The waves have now been divided into sub "themes", which means the following:
    • Wave 1-25 - Normal
    • Wave 26-50 - Difficult
    • Wave 51-75 - Extreme
    • Wave 76-100 - Death
    • Wave 100+ - Wasteland

Each sub theme will hold different mobs and challenges, many new mobs have been added, but some have also received major changes to match their tier level.

It should be noted that Wasteland is considered "unreachable" by most players and will not have any more bosses or special events after wave 100. Feel free to push your limits though, as mob scaling will be exponential from here on out.

Weapon Balance

Gradient
  • Added 8% speed bonus

Class Balance

General
  • All classes with abilities that deal max health damage now have a global cap of minimum 100 damage and max 500 damage. (Can be increased by damage modifiers.)

For reference: if the % health value is below 100 it will automatically be set to 100, vice versa if the % health value is above 500. Damage modifiers can increase this cap. For example, a damage modifier of 20% will increase the maximum cap to 600. This will affect the following abilities:

  • Fireball
  • Wounding Strike
  • Avenger's Strike
  • Windfury
  • Healing Rain
  • Undying Army
  • Draining Miasma

Assassin's Death strike execute threshold will remain uncapped.

Pyromancer
  • Flame Awakening (Flame Burst Master) no longer increases energy cost.

Cryomancer
  • Ice Barrier will now take aggro every second instead of just once.

Aquamancer
  • Typhoon (Water Breath Master) now reduces allies' cooldowns by 3s and are healed for 2% of their max health for 5 seconds instead of 1%.

Thunderlord
  • Chain Lightning's energy cost upgrade branch has been changed from +2 bounces to +1 bounce at tier 4.
  • Shredding Fury (Windfury Master) now deals 0.5% of their max health as bonus damage instead of 100% additional weapon damage.
  • Thunderbolt's (Lightning Rod Master) damage boost no longer stacks with itself.
  • Capacitor Totem's impair upgrade branch has been changed from 10/20/30/40% to 7.5/15/22.5/30% and no longer grants cooldown reduction.
  • Capacitor Totem's spark upgrade branch has been changed from 0.5/1/1.5/2 blocks radius to 5/10/15/20% cooldown reduction.

Spiritguard
  • Fallen Souls' damage upgrade branch has been changed from 7.5/15/22.5/30% to 15/30/45/60%.
  • Increased max active Soulbinding Weapon instances from 2 to 3.
  • Revengeance (Repentance Master) now also increases energy conversion based on damage taken by 50%.
  • Death's Debt base damage taken after the totem ends has been reduced from 100% to 50%, the master upgrade will further reduce this by 25%.
  • Ghoul's Gamble (Death's Debt Master) now grants you 80% knockback resistance during Spirits' Respite.

Berserker
  • Wounding Strike's damage upgrade branch has been changed from 7.5/15/22.5/30% to 10/20/30/40%.
  • Seismic Smash's (Seismic Wave Master) damage multiplier has been reduced from 2x to 1.5x at max distance.
  • Seismic Wave's damage upgrade branch has been changed from 7.5/15/22.5/30% to 5/10/15/20%.
  • Ground Slam's damage upgrade branch has been changed from 7.5/15/22.5/30% to 5/10/15/20%.
  • Berserk's damage boost no longer stacks with itself.

Defender
  • Lacerating Strike (Wounding Strike Master) now grants the same effect to allies within 6 blocks of the struck enemy.
  • Changed several mob attacks to not apply knockback to the Defender when Intervening someone.
  • Final Stand (Last Stand Master) will no longer periodically knock enemies back but is now triggered by re-activating the ability or using shift. The Defender is granted 50% knockback resistance for the duration of Last Stand.

Avenger
  • Avenger's Slash (Avenger's Strike Master) now deals 0.5% of the enemies' max health as bonus damage against ELITE enemies.

Crusader
  • Transcendent Presence (Inspiring Presence Master) now only reduces cooldowns by 30s instead of fully resetting them.

Protector
  • Hammer of Illusion (Hammer of Light Master) can no longer hit critical hits.

Assassin
  • Death Strike (Judgement Strike Master) now executes enemies below 30% health instead of 25%.
  • Soul Switch's base cooldown has been lowered by 25%.
  • Soul Switch now spawns a decoy that aggros enemies and deals damage by default. The master upgrade will now double the damage and apply a crippling debuff to all enemies hit. You will also be healed for 10% of your missing health upon swapping if unlocked.

Vindicator
  • Righteous Slash (Righteous Strike Master) no longer knocks enemies back but now silences your initial strike target for 10 seconds. Additional targets still get silenced for 4 seconds.
  • Soul Shackle can no longer target no enemies if the master upgrade is unlocked.
  • Deflective Tesseract (Prism Guard Master) no longer doubles duration, as it wasn't meant to in the first place.
  • Reflective Barrier (Vindicate Master) now ignores passive damage resistance when reflecting damage.

Apothecary
  • Impaling Slash (Impaling Strike Master) now deals triple damage against targets affected by LEECH instead of double.
