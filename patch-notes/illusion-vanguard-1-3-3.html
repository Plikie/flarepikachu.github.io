Illusion Vanguard Hotfix - v1.3.3
Released December 8th 2022
Wave Defense
General

  • Fixed guild tag not updating for new guild players
  • Added 'coins converted' stat to players in guild menu

Class Balance

Berserker
  • Changed BloodLust to cap total healing based on the player's max HP.

Aquamancer
  • Players with an active ability effect from Water Breath will not get their cooldowns reduced by another Water Breath.

Weapon Balance

Fervent
  • The Fervent weapon ability now lasts 45s instead of 30s.

Mob Balance

Void Lancer
  • Changed Void Cleanse to take duration from BUFF timers instead of ability timers. (This was the original intended behaviour.)

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed leaderboards removing some players.
  • Fixed being unable to reinvite a player to a guild if 5 minutes pass.
